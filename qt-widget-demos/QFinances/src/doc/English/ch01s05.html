It is here possible to change a program language. And also there are two insets one for tuning of courses of currencies other for the change of parameters of account: establishment/changes a password, change of currency of account.

Important
At the change of currency of account converting does not take a place.

On saying nothing the accessible followings currencies: $, Euro, Grv, it is possible to add new currencies with establishment of course in relation to main currency.

  1. – adds new currency.

  2. – deletes the chosen currency from a list. (deleting main currency is not possible, it at first must be displaced).

  3. – changes a currency chosen exchange rate. (changing a currency main exchange rate is impossible, he always on saying nothing 1).

  4. – sets the chosen currency main.
